Servicing

What a service includes generally varies depending on the make and model of your vehicle. Typically a Standard Service includes changing the oil & filter and various visual checks to ensure your vehicle is in the best condition possible. Our Full Service includes the standard service in addition to changing of the air filter, spark plugs, pollen filter and fuel filter along with several other visual checks. There is also a Basic Service which includes changing the oil & filter and topping up under bonnet fluids. The full list of typically performed checks and services for both service types are shown below.


The prices below are based on a Ford Fiesta 1.25L 2004 petrol vehicle and are to be used as a baseline as the price will vary depending on the make and model of your car.
